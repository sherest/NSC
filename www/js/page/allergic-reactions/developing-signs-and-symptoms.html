Developing Signs and Symptoms

  • Anxiety, agitation
  • Feeling that the throat is closing and chest becoming tight
  • Rapid, weak pulse; low blood pressure
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Severe respiratory distress: coughing, wheezing, or hoarseness
  • Altered mental status
  • Severe headache
  • Weakness or dizziness
  • Pale or ashen skin or cyanosis
