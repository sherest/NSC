Signs and Symptoms of Heat Exhaustion

  • Sweating, pale or ashen, moist skin (often cool)
  • Thirst  
  • Fatigue, weakness, exhaustion  
  • Muscle cramps  
  • Later signs and symptoms:
    • Headache, dizziness, fainting  
    • Nausea, vomiting  
    • Fast, shallow breathing  
    • Rapid heart rate
