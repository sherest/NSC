Signs and Symptoms of Respiratory Distress

  • Gasping for air
  • Panting
  • Breathing faster or slower than normal
  • Wheezing or other sounds with breathing
  • Using accessory muscles
  • Inability to speak a full sentence without pausing to breathe
  • Skin is pale, cool, and moist; lips and nail beds are bluish (cyanosis)
  • Dizziness or disorientation
  • Feelings of extreme distress
  • Sitting and leaning forward, hands on knees
