Emergency Care for Altered Mental Status

  1. Perform standard patient care.
  2. Determine nature of the problem if possible. With responsive patient, take SAMPLE history. See here
  3. Place unresponsive patient in recovery position unless spinal injury suspected.
  4. Have suction available.
  5. Consider use of an airway adjunct to protect the airway. See also AVPUunder Abbreviations and Memory Aids
