Jaw Thrust

With possible spinal injury, do not tilt head back. Only lift jaw upward, using both hands.

  • Jaw thrust is more difficult and fatiguing but is effective.
  • If the lips close, open lower lip with your gloved thumb.
  • When in doubt about a possible spinal injury, use the jaw thrust.
  • If jaw thrust does not successfully open the airway, then switch to head tilt–chin lift.
