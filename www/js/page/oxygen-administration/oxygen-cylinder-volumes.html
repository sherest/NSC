Oxygen cylinder volumes

  1. Check equipment and labels on cylinder and regulator, and prepare the tubing and delivery device.
  2. Remove any protective seal, point the cylinder opening away, and open the main valve for 1 second.
  3. Remove any protective seals and attach the regulator to the oxygen cylinder.
  4. Open the cylinder valve.
  5. Check the pressure regulator gauge.
  6. Attach tubing to a flowmeter and oxygen delivery device.
  7. Set the flowmeter at the correct oxygen flow rate:
    • 1-6 L/min for a nasal cannula
    • 10 L/min for a face mask
    • 10-15 L/min for a bag mask or non-rebreather mask
  8. Confirm oxygen is flowing.
  9. Position the delivery device on the patient and continue ventilations (or allow the patient to breathe spontaneously).
  10. Monitor the pressure gauge and remove the delivery device and change tanks if pressure drops below 500 psi.
