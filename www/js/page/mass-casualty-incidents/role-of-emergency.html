Mass-Casualty Incidents

Role of Emergency Medical Responders in MCIs

  • If you are the first to respond, recognize the emergency as an MCI, report it immediately and request assistance.
  • The first knowledgeable EMS provider on scene becomes the triage officer, until relieved by a responder with more training.
  • Perform triage first on all patients.
  • Available personnel and equipment are first directed to highest-priority patients.

On Arrival at an MCI

  • Report to the staging section, if present, or to the command post.
  • Identify yourself and your level of training and follow directions from Section Chief or Incident Commander.
  • Report immediately to assigned team and the designated individual in charge.
  • Perform only the tasks you are assigned.
  • When you complete assigned tasks, report back for a new assignment.
  • Act only on direction of supervisory personnel.
