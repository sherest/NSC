Nasal Airway Insertion in Adults

  1. Choose correct nasal airway size, measuring from the nostril to the tip of the earlobe. The diameter of airway should not be so large that tissue around nostril turns white from the pressure.
  2. Coat nasal airway with water-soluble lubricant.
  3. Insert nasal airway in right nostril with bevel toward the base of nostril or septum.
  4. Insert nasal airway straight back, sliding it along floor of the nostril. If it cannot be inserted into one nostril, try the other; do not force it.
  5. Insert nasal airway until flange rests against the nose.
