Scene Size-Up

  • En route to the scene, consider safety factors for you, bystanders, and the patient.
  • Dispatcher’s information may alert you to hazards or special precautions.
  • As you arrive, begin scene size-up before exiting the vehicle and while approaching the patient, including:
    • Appropriate BSI precautions to take  
    • Any hazards at the scene  
    • Clues to help you better understand the nature of the emergency

Vehicle Crash Hazards

  • Traffic hazards  
  • Downed wires  
  • Risk of fire or explosion  
  • Unstable vehicles  
  • Hazardous materials

Potential Violence

  • Crime scenes  
  • Potentially violent patient or bystanders  
  • Guard dogs, wild animals

Environmental Dangers

  • Unstable surfaces  
  • Water, ice  
  • Weather extremes
