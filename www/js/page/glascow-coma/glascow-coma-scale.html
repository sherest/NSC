Glascow Coma Scale

Opens eyes:

4 – Spontaneously

3 – To voice command

2 – To pain

1 – Not at all

Responds verbally:

5 – Appropriately (oriented)

4 – Confused

3 – Inappropriate words

2 – Incomprehensible sounds

1 – Not at all

Responds with movement (motor response):

6 – On command

5 – To localized painful stimulus

4 – Withdraws from pain

3 – Flexion on painful stimulus

2 – Extension on painful stimulus

1 – Not at all

Scoring:

13-15 Mild head injury

9-12 Moderate injury

3-8 Severe injury
