Correcting Errors in a Report

  • Do not attempt to erase or blot out an error.
  • If possible, use a different color ink to make corrections.
  • Draw a single line through the error, initial it, and write the correct information beside it.
  • If correcting an error at later time, add the date and time of correction with your initials.
  • If adding information originally not noted in the report, add the note along with date and time and your initials.
