Bronchodilator (Inhaler) for an Asthma Attack

  1. Follow local protocol to assist the patient in using asthma medication (usually a metered dose inhaler) when:
    • Patient confirms it is an asthma attack.
    • Patient identifies the inhaler as his or her prescribed asthma medication.
    • Patient cannot self-administer the medication.
  2. Help the patient rest in position for easiest breathing.
  3. If needed, patient may use the inhaler again as prescribed or directed by his or her medical provider.
