AVPU Scale for Assessing Responsiveness

A
Alert
Patient aware of the time and place
V
Responds to Verbal stimuli
but is not clearly oriented to time and place
P
Responds to Painful stimuli
Moves or responds to an irritating stimulus but not to verbal stimuli; gently pinch skin between neck and shoulder to elicit response
U
Unresponsive to all stimuli
Eyes are closed, and no movement or response to painful stimuli

(See Assess Mental Status)
