When a Victim Refuses Consent

  • Follow local protocol. When in doubt, err in favor of providing care.
  • Do not argue with the victim or question the victim’s personal beliefs.
  • Do not try to give care. Touching a victim who refuses care may make you guilty of battery.
  • Wait for responding EMS units to arrive and evaluate situation. Do not make independent decision regarding refusal of care.
  • If possible, have the victim sign a witnessed refusal form or have a witness hear the victim’s refusal.
