Falsification of Records

  • Errors of omission or commission must be documented.
  • Any intentional omission or misreporting in written documentation is illegal and unethical.

Ethical Responsibilities

  • As an emergency medical responder, you accept ethical responsibility to provide the best care possible.
  • Make the physical and emotional needs of the patient your highest priority.
  • Practice your caregiving skills until you are certain you have mastered all skills.
  • Regularly attend continuing education and refresher programs to learn new information and skills and to ensure continued mastery of skills.
  • After each patient care episode, review your performances to see if you can improve your response time, give better care, or communicate more effectively.
  • Be honest in your reporting and documentation.
