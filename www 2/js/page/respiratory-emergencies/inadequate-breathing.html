Inadequate Breathing

  • Inadequate breathing is breathing too slowly (less than 10 breaths/minute) or too weakly.
  • Patient likely has altered mental status and may have cyanotic appearance.
  • Patient needs ventilations.
  • Do not wait for respiratory arrest before providing ventilations for an adult who is not breathing adequately.
  • An infant or child may have a pulse of 60 or higher but still be breathing inadequately and need ventilation.
